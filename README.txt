Galaga for GBA

The objective is simple: kill the enemies and avoid being killed yourself. You have to watch out for a few things however; if you collide with an enemy or an asteroid, 
you'll lose a life! 

Once you've started the game, simply press start to begin. The controls are simple:
-use the d-pad to move around and avoid enemies and asteroids
-use the a button to shoot enemies

Once you score 100 points, you win! Keep in mind, if you lose all of your lives, it's game over.
